## 5. Comprehensive Coverage Table

### 5.1 Coverage Analysis Framework

For each coverage, document:
- **Coverage Name**: Official policy language name
- **Coverage Type**: First Party / Third Party / Regulatory / Crime
- **Limit**: Dollar amount available
- **Sublimit**: If less than aggregate
- **Deductible/Retention**: Amount insured pays first
- **Waiting Period**: For time-element coverages
- **Period of Restoration**: Maximum recovery period
- **Payment Basis**: Pay on Behalf vs. Reimbursement
- **Prior Acts**: Full Prior Acts / Limited / None
- **Maturity Score**: 1-10 rating
- **Special Conditions**: Panel requirements, consent provisions, etc.
